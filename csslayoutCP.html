    • INTRODUCING GOMYCODE

    GoMyCode is changing the education market by training talents in a dynamic belnded environement on digital skills and connects them with tech opportunities around the world. Based on 4 Main Aspects: - Data based Assessment - Data based matching - Business Analytics - Learning Platform - Content Editor GoMyCode provides digital Skills Training for the Future of Work. In 4 Different fields: - DATA - DEVELOPMENT - DESIGN - DIGITAL MARKETING

    HISTORY

    GOMYCODE was established in 2017 on account of our enthusiasm for the digital world, and to connect the gap that exists between what the standard training offered and what the accessible job offers on the market. We have established a genuine network around advanced innovation and we are glad to be pioneers in this field. After opening its doors for the first time in a coworking space, GOMYCODE won the BlooMMasters program in MIT (in 2017) and opened its first hackerspace in Tunis before expanding to Sousse, Sfax, Nabeul, El Menzah V, Beja, Gafsa and Gabes. During its initial 3 years, GOMYCODE trained more than 10 000 students and set up associations with no less than 100 partners among private and public organizations, colleges and non-administrative associations. In 2020, GOMYCODE is thinking big and starting its first activities outside of Tunisia. Notably in Algeria, France, Morocco, Egypt, Bahrain, Nigeria and Côte d'Ivoire.

    VALUE

    Education and new digital technologies are walking hand in hand nowadays, that’s why we firmly believe in our process. We offer an interesting learning strategy consolidating Online and Offline Learning permitting students to advance at their own pace. Students are mentored and observed by our instructors to guarantee their progress all through the program. We put resources into the learning experience of our understudies by giving a dynamic and "disruptive" climate for them to build up their aptitudes and skills. We offer you the chance to learn in a field that captivates you. Everything goes at your own pace and according to your availability to discover your place in a constantly evolving world. This is only the beginning !

    FUTURE

    Our goal is to be a player in the world of tomorrow in terms of education and employment. And what better tool for that than the digital. We give everyone the opportunity to acquire new skills and cutting-edge knowledge. We aim to grow our community globally, with more than 50 hackerspaces around the world with a focus on the MENA region, thereby making education in new technologies accessible to everyone.
